Frequently Asked Questions

When should I register for VAT as a creator?

You must register once your taxable turnover exceeds £90,000 in a rolling 12-month period. We’ll help you determine the optimal timing and handle the registration process.

Should I operate as a sole trader or limited company?

Sole trader is simpler under £30k-40k annually. Limited company becomes more tax-efficient above that threshold, typically saving £10k-20k+ yearly. We provide free assessments.

What expenses can I claim?

Allowable: Camera/audio equipment, editing software, props, home office costs (portion of rent/utilities), travel for content creation, marketing.

Not allowable: Personal clothing (unless costume-specific), regular meals, personal entertainment subscriptions.

How do you handle sponsorship income?

Sponsorships are fully taxable trading income. We provide invoice templates, set aside 30-40% automatically for taxes, track related expenses, and properly declare gifted products.

Can you help with US tax forms?

Yes! We file W-8BEN to reduce withholding to 0%, handle Form 8833 for treaty benefits, reconcile 1042-S forms, and claim foreign tax credits on UK returns.

How quickly can I get started?

Most creators are fully onboarded within 48 hours. We connect your platforms, import historical data, set up your dashboard, and begin real-time tracking immediately.

VAT Registration for E-commerce: When and How

Learn when you need to register for VAT as an e-commerce seller and how to navigate the registration process.

Understanding VAT registration is crucial for e-commerce businesses. Here's everything you need to know.

When Do You Need to Register?

You must register for VAT when your taxable turnover exceeds £90,000 in a 12-month period. However, voluntary registration can benefit some businesses.

Benefits of Early Registration

Even below the threshold, registering for VAT allows you to reclaim VAT on business expenses and can make your business appear more established.

Special Schemes for E-commerce

If you sell to EU customers, you may need to register for the One Stop Shop (OSS) scheme. Amazon FBA sellers have specific VAT considerations.

Making Tax Digital (MTD)

All VAT-registered businesses must use MTD-compatible software for VAT returns. We can help you set this up.
